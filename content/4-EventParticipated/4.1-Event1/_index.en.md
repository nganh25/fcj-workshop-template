# GenAI-powered App-DB Modernization Workshop

## Event Information

- **Time:** 09:00, August 13, 2025
- **Location:** 26th Floor, Bitexco Financial Tower, Ho Chi Minh City
- **Role:** Participant

## Objectives

- Learn approaches for application and database modernization.
- Explore Domain-Driven Design and event-driven architecture.
- Compare EC2, containers, Fargate, and Lambda.
- Learn about Amazon Q Developer and code-transformation tools.

## Highlights

### Domain-Driven Design

The session explained how to start from the business domain, build a ubiquitous language, identify domain events, and divide a system into bounded contexts.

### Event-Driven Architecture

Publish/Subscribe, Point-to-Point, and Streaming patterns can reduce service coupling, improve scalability, and increase resilience.

### Compute Evolution

The workshop compared responsibility models from EC2 and ECS to Fargate and Lambda, helping attendees choose between virtual machines, containers, and functions.

### Amazon Q Developer

Amazon Q Developer can support multiple SDLC stages, including analysis, coding, testing, Java/.NET upgrades, and modernization.

## Application to the Project

- Design around business capabilities rather than technology alone.
- Use events for long-running or non-blocking tasks.
- Select compute services based on workload, operations, and cost.
- Apply these ideas to Step Functions, SQS, EventBridge, and Lambda in Smart Attendance SaaS.
